About CoastSense

Protecting coastal communities through innovative technology and real-time hazard monitoring

Our Mission

CoastSense is dedicated to safeguarding coastal communities by providing cutting-edge technology for real-time ocean hazard monitoring and early warning systems. We combine citizen science, advanced analytics, and emergency response coordination to protect lives and property along India's extensive coastline.
