Frequently Asked Questions

How do I report a coastal hazard? +
You can report coastal hazards through our mobile app, web platform, or by calling our emergency hotline at +91-1800-XXX-XXXX. Include details about the location, type of hazard, and any immediate observations.
How quickly do you respond to reports? +
High-severity reports receive immediate response within 15 minutes. Medium-severity reports are addressed within 1 hour, and low-severity reports are reviewed within 4 hours. All reports are monitored 24/7.
Can I access real-time ocean data? +
Yes! Our dashboard provides real-time ocean monitoring data including wave heights, sea surface temperatures, and current speeds. Register for a free account to access detailed analytics and historical data.
How can my organization partner with CoastSense? +
We welcome partnerships with coastal communities, research institutions, and emergency services. Contact our partnerships team at partnerships@coastsense.in to discuss collaboration opportunities.
Is CoastSense data available for research? +
Yes, we provide anonymized data for research purposes under our data sharing agreement. Academic institutions and approved research organizations can apply for data access through our research portal.
